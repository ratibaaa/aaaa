Booking & Flights

Q: How do I change my flight?
A: You can change your flight up to 24 hours before departure by contacting customer support at +213 792 461 160.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: More than 7 days before departure: Full refund
3-7 days before departure: 50% refund
Less than 3 days: No refund (can reschedule)

Q: Are meals included?
A: Yes, all SKYLURE bookings include complimentary meals and beverages.

Q: How do I get my boarding pass?
A: Boarding passes are available for download 24 hours before departure via email or by contacting us.

Baggage

Q: What is the baggage allowance?
A: Economy: 23kg checked + 8kg cabin
Business: 32kg checked x2 + 8kg cabin x2
