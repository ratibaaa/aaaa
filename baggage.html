Baggage Policy

Travel light, travel right

Economy Class

  • 1 checked bag (23kg maximum)
  • 1 cabin bag (8kg maximum, 55x40x20cm)
  • 1 personal item (handbag/laptop)

Business Class

  • 2 checked bags (32kg each maximum)
  • 2 cabin bags (8kg each maximum)
  • 1 personal item

Important Information

Additional Baggage: Can be purchased during booking or at airport

Sports Equipment: Special allowances apply

Medical Equipment: Exempt from weight limits

Fragile Items: Must be properly packed and declared
